Eezpense Privacy Policy

Effective Date:July 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Eezpense! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when using our app.

Data We Collect

Currently, Eezpense does not collect or store any personal data from users. All expense records are stored directly on your device in a local database. We do not transmit or share your expense information with any third parties.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your expense data stays on your device. We recommend backing up your data if needed, as we do not have control over your local device security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any personal data because we do not collect any.

Your Privacy Rights

Since your data is stored locally, you are responsible for managing and backing up your records.

Security Measures

Your data remains on your device, and we do not have access to it. Ensure your device is secure to protect your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here.
